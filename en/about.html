Our Story

A company forged in the industry of Navarre

Founded in 1993 in Bera, Navarre, UBELA S.L. was born from the industrial vocation of the Basque-Navarrese region. What began as a small machining workshop has grown, over the years, into a consolidated regional reference.

With more than 30 years of experience, we have specialised in the manufacture of dies, stamps, lamination rollers and custom tooling for the automotive, railway and steel industry sectors. Every part that leaves our facilities carries the culture of work well done.

Our philosophy is simple: listen to the customer, understand their production process and manufacture the most precise and durable solution possible. No shortcuts. No compromises on quality.

History

COMPANY MILESTONES

Three decades of steady growth, technology investment and market expansion.

1993

Founded

UBELA S.L. opens its doors in Bera, Navarre. A small machining workshop with great ambitions and a commitment to manufacturing with precision from day one.

2000

Facility Expansion

Growing demand leads us to expand the industrial unit, doubling the working floor area and enabling the addition of new machines and specialist staff.

2010

CNC Lathes Incorporated

Strategic investment in state-of-the-art CNC machining centres. The technological leap allows us to handle more complex projects with micrometric tolerances and optimised cycle times.

2020

New Railway Markets

Opening of the railway sector as a new line of business. We begin supplying machined components and welded structures to manufacturers and integrators in the rail transport sector.

2024

Revenue Growth +50%

Year closed with 50% revenue growth compared to the previous year. A result that reflects our customers' confidence and the strength of our production model.
