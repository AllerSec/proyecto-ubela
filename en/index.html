What We Do

Our Services

Dies & Stamps

Precision manufacturing of dies and stamps for high-volume industrial processes, guaranteeing dimensional accuracy and durability.

Lamination Rollers

Design and manufacture of lamination rollers for the steel and metallurgical industry, with rigorous surface finishes and tight tolerances.

Industrial Metalwork

Custom metal fabrication and structural solutions for industrial equipment, adapted to the specific requirements of each project.

About Us

Three decades of industrial precision

Based in Bera, Navarre, UBELA S.L. has been a reference in precision machining since 1993. Our team of experienced engineers and machinists works with state-of-the-art equipment to deliver components that meet the most demanding specifications.

We work with leading companies in the automotive, railway and steel industry sectors, providing technical solutions that combine craftsmanship with modern manufacturing precision.
