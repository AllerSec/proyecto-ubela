01

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, phone or tablet) when you visit a website. They help sites work correctly, improve efficiency and provide information to site owners. Cookies do not harm your device and do not contain viruses.

Strictly necessary cookies activate automatically. All other cookies are only activated with your explicit consent via our cookie banner.

02

Types of cookies

  • By origin: first-party (set by ubela.net) or third-party (set by external services such as Google Analytics).
  • By duration: session cookies (deleted when you close the browser) or persistent cookies (remain for a set period).
  • By purpose: strictly necessary (essential for site function), analytical (measure usage, require consent), preference (remember your choices, require consent), or advertising (not used by UBELA S.L.).

04

Third-party cookies

This site uses Google Analytics via Google Tag Manager. Google may combine this data with other information it holds about you, per its own privacy policy.

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Analytics opt-out add-on

UBELA S.L. does not use advertising or social media cookies from third parties.

05

Managing your cookie preferences

  • Site preferences panel: on your first visit a cookie banner allows you to accept or reject non-necessary cookies. You can change your choice at any time.
  • Browser settings: all modern browsers allow you to control cookie behaviour (see below).
  • Opt-out tools: you can use the Google Analytics opt-out add-on to disable tracking across all websites.

Important: disabling technical cookies may affect the website's correct functioning. Disabling analytics cookies does not affect browsing.
