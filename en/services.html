Technical Capabilities

WHAT WE MANUFACTURE

Dies & Stamps

Manufacturing of forming dies and cutting and stamping punches for the automotive and metallurgical industries. We design and machine every part to micrometric tolerances, using high-toughness tool steels and specific heat treatments to guarantee maximum service life in series production.

Lamination Rollers

High-precision rollers for cold and hot rolling lines, manufactured from special steels selected according to the customer's production process. Final grinding with controlled Ra roughness values, dynamic balancing and full dimensional verification before each delivery.

Industrial Metalwork

Heavy metalwork: welded steel structures, pressure vessels and containers, industrial frames and large-format welded assemblies. We apply certified welding processes (MIG/MAG, TIG, electrode) and carry out tightness tests and weld inspection using non-destructive testing.

Cutting Blades

Industrial blades for metal sheet, paper, plastic and other material cutting lines. Manufactured from heat-treated tool steel (hardening and tempering) and precision-ground to guarantee an optimal cutting edge and long service life. Reconditioning and resharpening service available.

Roller Conveyors

Internal transport systems for production lines, manufactured in stainless or carbon steel according to the application. We design and build straight, curved and motorised roller conveyors, adapted to the specific dimensions and loads of the customer's production process.

General Tooling

Design and manufacture of custom tooling for manufacturing and quality-control processes: welding jigs, inspection gauges, machining fixtures, assembly tools and production accessories. We adapt to any specific customer requirement with tight delivery schedules.

Methodology

OUR PROCESS

From initial enquiry to finished part, every project follows a rigorous process aimed at total quality.

01

Analysis

Review of drawings, technical specifications and customer requirements. Evaluation of optimal materials and processes.

02

Design

CAD/CAM modelling, CNC programming and machining operation planning. Internal review and validation.

03

Machining

Manufacturing at our facilities using state-of-the-art CNC machinery. In-process dimensional control.

04

Delivery

Final inspection, technical documentation and on-time delivery. After-sales service and part warranty.
