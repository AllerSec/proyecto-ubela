01

Data controller

The data controller is UBELA S.L., VAT B31419146, Calle Zalain Industrialdea, 9, 31780 Bera (Navarra), Spain. Contact: juanma@ubela.net / +34 948 63 11 67.

UBELA S.L. processes personal data provided through this website in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 (LOPDGDD).

02

Purposes and legal basis

  • Handling contact and quote requests: processing data submitted via the contact form. Legal basis: consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR) and legitimate interest (Art. 6.1.f GDPR).
  • Managing commercial relationships: execution of machining service contracts. Legal basis: performance of a contract (Art. 6.1.b GDPR).
  • Legal obligations: retention of invoices and accounting records. Legal basis: legal obligation (Art. 6.1.c GDPR).
  • Website analytics: Google Analytics via Google Tag Manager to improve our services. Legal basis: consent (Art. 6.1.a GDPR) obtained via the cookie banner.

We do not carry out automated decision-making or profiling.

03

Categories of data processed

  • Identity data: name, company name, job title.
  • Contact data: email address, phone number, postal address.
  • Request data: description of industrial requirements, technical drawings or specifications voluntarily provided.
  • Financial data: bank or billing details, only for clients within the contractual relationship.
  • Browsing data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, obtained via analytics cookies with your prior consent.

We do not process special categories of data (health, ethnicity, beliefs, etc.) and ask that you do not provide such information.

04

Retention periods

  • Contact requests without subsequent commercial relationship: 12 months from last communication.
  • Client data (active contract): during the commercial relationship and, once concluded, 5 years to fulfil tax and commercial obligations.
  • Accounting and invoicing data: 6 years under the Spanish Commercial Code.
  • Browsing data (analytics cookies): maximum 26 months per Google Analytics retention policy.
05

Recipients and international transfers

UBELA S.L. does not share your data with third parties except:

  • Technology service providers acting as data processors (web hosting, Google Analytics) under data processing agreements with adequate safeguards.
  • Public authorities when required by law (tax authorities, social security, etc.).

Google Analytics is configured with IP anonymisation. Google LLC participates in the EU–US Data Privacy Framework, ensuring an equivalent level of protection. No other international data transfers outside the EEA are made.

06

Your rights

Access
Confirm whether we process your data and obtain a copy.
Rectification
Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
Erasure
Request deletion of your data when no longer necessary.
Restriction
Request suspension of processing in certain circumstances.
Portability
Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Objection
Object to processing based on our legitimate interest.

To exercise any of these rights, write to juanma@ubela.net attaching a copy of your ID. We will respond within one month. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Authority (AEPD): www.aepd.es.

07

Minors

UBELA S.L.'s website and services are directed at industrial companies and professionals. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 14. If you believe a minor has provided us with data, please contact us immediately.

08

Security

UBELA S.L. has implemented appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against alteration, loss, unauthorised processing or access. Measures include HTTPS/TLS encryption, access controls, and regular security reviews. No transmission system is completely secure; if you suspect a breach, please notify us at juanma@ubela.net.

09

Changes to this policy

UBELA S.L. may update this privacy policy to reflect legislative changes or updates to our website. Changes will be published on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the website after changes are published constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
